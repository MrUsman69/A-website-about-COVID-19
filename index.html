About Corona Virus

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Most people who fall sick with COVID-19 will experience mild to moderate symptoms and recover without special treatment. However, some will become seriously ill and require medical attention.

see  More

How it Spreads.

The virus can spread from an infected person’s mouth or nose in small liquid particles when they cough, sneeze, speak, sing or breathe. These particles range from larger respiratory droplets to smaller aerosols.

How to Protect Yourself

Here are some little but important steps and information about how to protect yourself from this catastrophic pandemic.

Wear Mask

Wear a mask. CDC recommends wearing a non-medical-grade, cloth face covering in public settings, to avoid any type of risk.

Wash Your Hands

Wash your hands frequently with soap and water, scrubbing for at least 20 seconds, or use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer.

Stay at Home

Stay at home, even if you are not feeling sick, as there is a lot danger that it catches you, & seek appropriate medical guidance if so.

Dr. Farah Karim

Dr. Karim, reading news articles nonstop to educate herself on the virus, has done her best to counsel her patients on practical steps to prevent exposure: using credit cards instead of cash, wearing gloves when going outside and carrying personal items in sandwich bags. “I wish I had taken sandwich bags to work and given everyone a sandwich bag,” she said.
