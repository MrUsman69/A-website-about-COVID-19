How to Protect Yourself

Here are some little but important steps and information about how to protect yourself from this catastrophic pandemic.

Wear Mask

Wear a mask. CDC recommends wearing a non-medical-grade, cloth face covering in public settings, to avoid any type of risk.

Wash Your Hands

Wash your hands frequently with soap and water, scrubbing for at least 20 seconds, or use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer.

Stay at Home

Stay at home, even if you are not feeling sick, as there is a lot danger that it catches you, & seek appropriate medical guidance if so.
